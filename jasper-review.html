Jasper's Standout Feature: Brand Voice

Jasper's brand voice feature learns your company's tone, terminology, and style from uploaded content. Once trained, every output matches your brand — something raw ChatGPT or cheaper tools can't do. For enterprise marketing teams managing multiple brands, this alone can justify the $69/mo Pro plan.

But for a solo blogger? Brand voice is overkill. You can achieve 80% of the same result by writing a good prompt in Writesonic or ChatGPT.

FAQ

Does Jasper have a free trial?

No. Jasper used to offer a 7-day free trial but removed it. There is no way to test Jasper without paying $49 for the first month.

Is Jasper better than Writesonic?

For enterprise teams with multiple brands: yes, Jasper's brand voice and collaboration tools are better. For everyone else, Writesonic at $20/mo with built-in SEO is the smarter buy.

Why did Jasper close its affiliate program?

Jasper closed its affiliate program in 2024, along with Copy.ai. Both companies shifted focus from content writers to enterprise sales teams, where affiliate marketing is less effective.

Final Verdict
