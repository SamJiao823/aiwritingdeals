Which Free AI Writer Should You Choose?

For most people, start with Rytr's free plan. 10,000 characters per month is enough for 5-10 blog posts or dozens of social media captions. Once you hit the limit, the Saver plan at $7.50/mo is the cheapest upgrade path in AI writing.

If you need GPT-4 quality, try Writesonic's 25 free credits to see the difference. Then decide whether the $20/mo Individual plan is worth the extra $12.50 over Rytr.

FAQ

Do any free AI writing tools require a credit card?

None of the top 3 (Rytr, Writesonic, Copy.ai) require a credit card for their free plans. ChatGPT Free also doesn't require one, but ChatGPT Plus does.

Which free AI writer has the highest output quality?

Writesonic's free credits use GPT-4, giving the best raw output quality. But Rytr's free plan gives you more total content per month at GPT-3.5 quality.

Can I use free AI writing tools for commercial content?

Yes — all four tools allow commercial use on their free plans. There are no restrictions on using AI-generated content for business purposes.
