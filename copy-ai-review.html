Who Should Use Copy.ai?

Copy.ai is now best for sales and marketing teams that need automated outbound workflows, not pure content writers. If you need blog posts, social media, or ad copy, Rytr or Writesonic deliver the same quality at a fraction of the price.

However, if you need AI-powered sales sequences, CRM enrichment, and GTM automation, Copy.ai's Pro plan at $36/mo is actually competitive against tools like Outreach or Salesloft.

FAQ

Does Copy.ai have a free trial?

Yes — the Free plan gives you 2,000 words/month with no credit card required. The Pro plan has no free trial beyond that.

Is Copy.ai better than ChatGPT for writing?

Copy.ai has more structured templates and workflows than raw ChatGPT, but for most writing tasks, ChatGPT Plus ($20/mo) is cheaper and equally capable.

Can I earn affiliate commissions from Copy.ai?

No. Copy.ai closed its affiliate program. If you're looking for affiliate income, Rytr (30% recurring) and Writesonic (30% lifetime) are much better options.
