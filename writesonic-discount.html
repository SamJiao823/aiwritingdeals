What Are Writesonic "Credits" — And How Far Do They Go?

Writesonic uses a credit system instead of word counts. Each AI generation costs a certain number of credits depending on the feature:

  • AI Article Writer 6.0 — ~5 credits per article (1000-1500 words)
  • Blog outline — 1 credit
  • Product description — 1 credit
  • SEO meta tags — 1 credit
  • Content rewriter — 1 credit per ~500 words

With 25 free credits, you can generate 3-5 full articles or 20+ shorter pieces. The Individual plan ($20/mo) gives you unlimited credits.

FAQ

Does Writesonic have a promo code for 2026?

Writesonic occasionally runs seasonal promotions (Black Friday, New Year), but there's no permanent promo code. The best deal is their free plan — 25 credits with no credit card — to test it fully before paying.

What happens when I run out of free credits?

You'll be prompted to upgrade to the Individual plan ($20/mo). Your existing content remains accessible. If you don't upgrade, you simply can't generate new AI content.

Is Writesonic cheaper than Jasper?

Yes — significantly. Writesonic Individual is $20/mo vs Jasper Creator at $49/mo (59% cheaper). And Writesonic includes SEO scoring built in, while Jasper requires a separate Surfer SEO subscription at $69/mo.

Can I cancel Writesonic anytime?

Yes, all Writesonic plans are month-to-month. You can cancel anytime. There's no long-term contract and no cancellation fee.

Is Writesonic good for long-form SEO content?

Yes — Writesonic's AI Article Writer 6.0 is specifically designed for long-form SEO blog posts. It includes competitor analysis, keyword optimization, and an SEO content scoring tool — all built into the editor.
